Meet the Family

We are a close-nit group and we love to treat each other and yourselves as family. To do this we would like to introduce you to some of our team leaders and give you a little information about them!

Sarah

Meet Sarah! She has been a team member for two years here at Alpine River Adventures and is a blast to work with. Oringinally from Arizona she has been enjoying the beautful weather up here in Jackson Hole Wyoming. She has been enjoying the sport of white water rafting for the last ten years and has a lot of expierence on the river. She also enjoys mountain biking and hiking in her spare time. She is currently getting her bachelors in biology but enjoys spending the summer in the sun out on the river with all of us!

Pete

What can we say about Pete? Well I think first and foremost he is a funny man. One rafting adventure with him and he'll have your whole boat laughing so hard you'll have to watch out for yellow water. He has been with us on the river for ten years and has loved every second of it. He also enjoys rock climbing in his spare time and is an avid outdoorsman. He is hoping to be able to join the forestry service and keep up this great life.

Jack

One look at Jack and you will see he is the definition of a river rat. He loves the water and most days you can find him out in the water. He loves white water kayaking and goes to the extreme with it. If you want a fun ride he is definently the guide for you. He is the son of the owner of Alpine River Adventures and has been rafting from the young age of 4. This is his dream job and he enjoys every second he spends on the river with all of you!
